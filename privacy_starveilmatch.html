Privacy Statement

Starveil Match does not collect, store, or share personal data.

Data collection

No personal information is collected. The app does not use analytics SDKs, ad networks, or tracking identifiers.

Photos access

Photos permission is requested only if you choose Save to Photos. This permission is used solely to write the selected card image into your photo library. The app does not access, read, or upload your existing photos.

Network

The app can be used offline. No account is required and no data is transmitted to a server.

Local storage

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.
